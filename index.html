Santa Claus Visit

Santa Claus will come to your home, put gifts you have left out into his bag and make a big entrance. Typical visits include pictures with Santa (everyone, not just the children), listening to what the children want for Christmas, reading of a Christmas story, Santa telling a story of when he was caught delivering presents one night many years ago ("T'was the Night before Christmas"). Then Santa will hand out presents, sit for pictures and wish everyone a very Merry Christmas before he departs back to the North Pole.
